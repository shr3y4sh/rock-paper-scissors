Welcome to Rock-Paper-Scissors by Shreyash

I probably won't have to tell you the rules of this game but for people living under the rock (wink) here you go:

  • There are two players in this game, you and me
  • You will have to select on of Rock Paper Scissors (in your mind)
  • I will do the same
  • We will both shout our choices at the same time.
  • Now depending on our choices following applies
    • Rock smashes Scissors: Rock wins
    • Scissors cuts Paper: Scissors win
    • Paper wraps Rock: Paper wins
  • We note down the winner and points, onto next round
  • Process repeats. The player who scores 5 points first, wins the game.
